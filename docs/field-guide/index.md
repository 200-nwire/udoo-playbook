# The Field Guide

**Understand the reason behind the practice, and you can adapt the practice.**

---

The [OS](/guide/start-here) tells you *what* teams must agree on. This book teaches you *how* to actually do the work — the craft behind the agreements.

Every chapter follows the same shape:

1. **The moment this chapter is for** — two sentences. You recognize yourself.
2. **The direct answer** — before any explanation.
3. **Why this works** — deep enough that you can adapt when your situation doesn't match.
4. **Step by step** — numbered, conditional where needed.
5. **Common mistakes** — described from the inside, not as warnings.
6. **From the Novel** — the same concept, lived by a character in [The Weight of Knowing](/novel/). The craft behind the scene.
7. **When this breaks down** — what conditions make this practice fail.

You open this book when you're in the middle of something. You scan for your situation. You find the answer first, then the reasoning.
