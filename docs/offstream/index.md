## Purpose

Offstream turns a working product into a **growing, sustainable business**. It is responsible for customer adoption, retention, expansion, and revenue.

Critically, Offstream is also the **most important source of real-world signal** that feeds back into Upstream. The things customers actually do (and avoid doing) are more valuable than any assumption in an Initiative Brief.

::: tip The most important sentence in this chapter
Offstream does not wait passively for features to ship. It engages from Day 1 of a release, shapes the market narrative, manages accounts, and surfaces opportunities that become tomorrow's Initiatives.
:::
